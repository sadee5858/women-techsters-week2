What are your favourite trends in the fashion industry, past or present?


00.05: Speaker 2

Some of my favourite trends are from the 1920s, a time when you see clean designs and simple symmetry in clothes. This usually reflects in my own designs, although I add some innovative elements and keep today's current trends in mind. I believe that our fashion sense is a result of the legacy trends and our unique personalities.
